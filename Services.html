Paige is the founder of Root to Rise Therapy.

Paige believes that all people, no matter their age, have the capacity to live a life aligned with their truest self. She trusts that everyone has the ability to reflect, grow, and move past unhelpful patterns and old choices. In therapy, Paige creates a safe environment that is warm, light, and free of judgement.

If you are a parent seeking a guide that can help your child feel more confident, successful calm, in touch with him/herself, Paige may be a good match for your child or adolescent. Paige specializes in working with children and teens, and she helps young people feel more centered and grounded in their bodies and lives. Paige provides a space for young people to be their honest selves, helping them to live with more intention and clarity in a world loaded with social media, school stress, and ever changing interpersonal dynamics.

Paige has been working with children, adolescents, and their families for over five years. Originally practicing in schools, Paige has extensive experience doing therapy with children and adolescents and understands many of the challenges that young people today face. She also specializes in working with families, children, and teens who have been touched by adoption.
