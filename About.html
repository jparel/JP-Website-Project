HI, I'M PAIGE!

A Licensed Professional Here to Guide You

Paige is a Licensed Graduate Professional Counselor. She received her Ed.M. in Counseling at Harvard University. Paige views her work from a client centered, attachment centered, and strengths based lens. She employs art based, somatic (body) based, play based, and talk based interventions in her practice and aims to best serve the needs of her clients. Paige also works with her clients in practicing mindfulness and is a Registered Yoga Teacher.
